Our Story

Nailed It Nail Studio was born from a dream and a love for all things sparkly! Founded in 2020 by nail artist extraordinaire Maya Chen, our studio started as a small passion project in her apartment.

Growing up in the early 2000s, Maya was obsessed with the Y2K aesthetic - the chrome finishes, holographic everything, and that unmistakable futuristic-yet-playful vibe. When she opened Nailed It, she knew exactly the atmosphere she wanted to create.

Today, we've grown into a full-service nail studio, but we've never lost that indie spirit. Every client who walks through our doors becomes part of our sparkly family!
