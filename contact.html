Booking Policies

📅 Appointments

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance, especially for weekends. Walk-ins are welcome based on availability!

💵 Deposits

A $20 deposit is required for appointments over $75 to secure your spot. This will be applied to your service total.

⏳ Cancellations

Please give us at least 24 hours notice if you need to reschedule. Late cancellations may forfeit the deposit.

💖 First Visit?

Please arrive 10 minutes early to fill out a quick client form. Feel free to bring nail inspo photos!
